ARTIST


What is Indian Art Haat?

Indian Art Haat is a marketplace that links artists directly with buyers.It has been created to allow indian folk artists to present and sell their work by themselves, simply and intuitively. The gallery offers buyers and collectors a direct relationship with sellers: privileged access to artists around the country, without intermediaries, and with prices direct from the artist's studio.


How can the Artist upload Artworks?

After registering and logging into Indian Art Haat:

  1. Go to your portal.
  2. Click on Add Artwork
  3. Upload the image of your artwork as per the image specifications and click on Update.

How to change your display picture?

After logging into Indian Art Haat, your display picture appears on the left side.To edit your display picture: :

  1. Click on the image.
  2. Select the upload new image option.
  3. Load the image from your computer or phone, and then crop it if necessary.


ART LOVER


How to get in touch with the artist?

indian Art Haat allows art lovers to directly contact the artists, for this redirect to the ARTIST page, choose the artist and contact him through his profile.


How to buy the original artwork?

Indian Art Haat puts art lovers in direct contact with the artists.To buy an artwork, use the button "ADD TO CART" and check out and make the payment at the payment gateway.


How to add reviews for a particular artwork?

After logging into Indian Art Haat and redirecting to the ARTWORK PAGE:

  1. Click on the image.
  2. Redirect on the Artwork Detail Page and click on Add Review.
  3. Add reviews.


MY ACCOUNT


How to create my account?

To do this, use the SIGN UP button at the top right of the pages of the site, then follow the instructions on the screen.


Is a Facebook/Twitter account necessary to register on Indian Art Haat?

No,Indian Art Haat actually integrates very well with Facebook for those who wish, but there is no obligation to use or own a Facebook account, it is completely optional.
