ABOUT US


Art has the power to make us think outside the box, ask questions, fascinate and inspire curiosity. Whether the medium is through paint, photography or design, art is something that surrounds us every day and is objective in taste.

Having identified a great opportunity to build the Madhubani Art market from the ground and taking the great work of artists and collectors forward,we got the idea of developing Indian Art Haat.
Indian Art Haat provides a platform to the Madhubani Artists all over India to showcase their art and attract masses of art lovers from all over the world. Through this we have tried to vanish the middleman in the selling of the artwork to the art lovers to ensure that the artist gets the right price for their hardwork.
For all the art connoisseurs out there,the site offers an index of Madhubani Paintings searchable by artist name,or most popular according to when it is uploaded or genre of the artwork, giving insight into the value of an artist at a given time and their marketing trends.

We provide the artists with a way to share their creations with others which may develop a love for Madhubani Paintings in the heart of the people all over the world.


What makes us unique?

Indian Art Haat is a marketplace that links artists directly with buyers.It has been created to allow indian folk artists to present and sell their work by themselves, simply and intuitively. The gallery offers buyers and collectors a direct relationship with sellers: privileged access to artists around the country, without intermediaries, and with prices direct from the artist's studio.
